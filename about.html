About Me

Jared started photography professionally in 2014, photographing weddings, families, kids and commercial events. Jared has an A.A.S. in Graphic Design from Penn Valley, where he fell in love with photography and honed his skills. Jared has a strong passion and creativity for photography and it shows in his work. When he isn’t snapping shutters, Jared enjoys spending time with his two rescued dogs, Stan and Elvis

Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever…It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything.
